Tesla SAFE Wash


Tesla Safe Wash is a safe and EV-friendly car wash solution for your beloved Tesla.

Our team of professionally trained and certified detailers will come to your location to wash and detail your Tesla, saving your valuable time and protecting your beloved Tesla from the risks of water damage and paint scrathes from automatic/tunnel car wash.

Regardless of numerous advantages over gasoline cars, EVs are much more sensitive to moisture. Water exposure from automatic car washes, tunnel car washes, or high-pressure car washes can lead to short circuits, electrical malfunctions, system failures, and even battery fires in EVs.


Tesla SAFE Wash is Better

No water damage risk

Eco-waterless Technologies

Tesla Safe Wash provides 100% handwash with an eco-friendly waterless car wash system and solutions. We use less than one champagne bottle of water per wash, eliminating the risk of water damage to moisture-sensitive electric vehicles.

Save Time & Charging

We Come to You

Our detailers come to your place at your service, so you don't have to leave the comfort of your home or office. You save time as well as the trip and charging wasted on car wash trip, waiting, and traffic.


No Body Damage Risk

100% Handwash Guaranteed

Our 100% handwash service, together with our eco-friendly solutions, provides the utmost gentle touch and 360-degree pampering care to your car. We eliminate the risk of scratches or body damage caused by car wash tunnel machineries.
